Embark on an epic journey across the cosmos in Kardashev Ascension - the ultimate sci-fi idle game that lets you build a civilization spanning the entire universe!

ASCEND THE KARDASHEV SCALE
Start as a fledgling planetary civilization harnessing wind and solar power. Progress through 5 distinct eras as you master increasingly powerful energy sources - from Dyson Spheres orbiting stars to harvesting the fabric of reality itself.

5 UNIQUE ERAS TO EXPLORE
- Planetary Era: Wind turbines, solar arrays, and fusion reactors
- Stellar Era: Dyson structures, star lifters, and stellar forges  
- Galactic Era: Neutron harvesters, Penrose spheres, cosmic string mines
- Universal Era: Vacuum extractors, dimensional rifts, dark energy wells
- Multiversal Era: Quantum foam siphons, brane colliders, the Omniversal Throne

RECRUIT COSMIC ARCHITECTS
Collect and deploy powerful Architects - legendary beings who boost your production and unlock special abilities. From brilliant scientists to ancient cosmic entities, each Architect brings unique bonuses to your civilization.

DEEP RESEARCH SYSTEM
Unlock over 50 technologies across multiple research trees. Discover new generators, unlock powerful upgrades, and push the boundaries of physics itself.

EXPEDITIONS & EXPLORATION  
Send your Architects on dangerous expeditions across the cosmos. Discover rare artifacts, earn Dark Matter, and uncover the secrets of the universe.

PRESTIGE & PROGRESSION
Reset your progress to earn Dark Matter and Dark Energy - powerful currencies that provide permanent bonuses. Each prestige cycle makes you stronger!

DAILY CHALLENGES & REWARDS
Complete daily and weekly challenges for bonus rewards. Log in each day to claim increasingly valuable prizes.

STUNNING VISUALS & AUDIO
Experience breathtaking cosmic visuals with era-specific themes. Each era features unique background music and ambient soundscapes that evolve as you progress.

- Idle gameplay - earn resources even when offline

- 15+ generators to build and upgrade
- 50+ research technologies to unlock

- Expedition system with risk/reward mechanics
- Prestige system with multiple currencies
- Daily login rewards with streak bonuses
- Daily and weekly challenges
- Cloud save support (coming soon)

- Fair free-to-play - no pay-to-win mechanics
